What is AMEE Hacks?

AMEE Hacks is the world’s best medical education hackathon. Our goal is to become the catalyst that drives technology innovation within the medical education industry. Powered by AMEE, we're bringing together the best developers, designers and medical experts from around the world for the purpose of building new products for medical education.

amee.org/conferences/amee-2015/hackathon

Submit your own challenge!

Are you facing problems with medical education? Dedicated teams of hackathon enthusiasts, instructional designers and medical educationalists will co-create technical solutions to identified problems, over a period of 48 hours.


As an example, the AMEE General Secretary’s Hackathon Challenge is: “How can learning be customised and tailored to the personal needs of the individual student as part of an adaptive learning programme?”

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a hackathon?
A hackathon is a place where designers, developers, and professionals come together to take an idea and turn it into a working prototype in 48 hours.

Can I apply as a team?
Unfortunately not. We are only accepting individual applications for AMEE Hacks.

What about travel and accommodation?
Accommodation will be provided on the Friday and Saturday night. Travel costs may be reimbursed on an individual basis. Please contact us directly for more information: medx@hackpartners.com.

Will there be free food & wifi?
Of course!

Will I receive any help during the hackathon?
Definitely. Thats what our mentors are for! During the hackathon, we will have design, development, and medical mentors floating inbetween teams.

Can I participate remotely?
Unfortunately not. We currently do not have the capacity to host remote teams, but we'll definitely look into this for AMEE Hacks 2016!

Who owns the IP?
The code and designs are owned by the individual team member who created them. The idea is not owned by anyone.

What do I need to bring?
Laptops, chargers, any gear that you might want to use during the hackathon, and sleeping bags!

How much does the event cost?
Application is free. If selected to participate, we will ask you to pay a £20 fee. This will go towards event costs and prizes.

Can you reimburse travel?
Selected participants can apply for reimbursement of up to £50 for UK residents, and £100 for EU/US residents.
